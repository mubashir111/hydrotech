Specialized Hydraulic Solutions

For detachable gooseneck (lowboy) trailers.

Hydrotech provides complete hydraulic solutions for detachable gooseneck trailers, enabling efficient front-loading of heavy and oversized equipment.

Our systems ensure the smooth operation of the gooseneck detachment and reattachment process. We supply high-pressure hydraulic cylinders, power units (diesel or electrical), and control systems designed to handle the massive loads associated with heavy haulage. Our solutions are compatible with various trailer designs and can be customized to meet specific operational speeds.

Key Features

  • High-pressure hydraulic cylinders
  • Independent engine-driven power packs
  • Rapid attach/detach mechanism support
  • Remote control operation options
  • Robust hose and fitting assemblies

Applications

  • Heavy Equipment Transport
  • Oversized Load Haulage
  • Construction Logistics
  • Oil & Gas Equipment Transport
